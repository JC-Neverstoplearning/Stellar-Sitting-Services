House Sitting

Having a house sitter can be a great comfort to someone who can't be at home. We have customers who travel for business, travel for pleasure, and sometimes need to be away for extended periods of time for family and/or medical reasons. Our goal is to care for your home so that you can be away without worries.

Our house sitting services include, but are not limited to:

  • General check-ins for security issues
  • Checking for plumbing and HVAC failures
  • Mail and package retrieval
  • Overnight stays if combined with pet care
  • Presence in the home when other service personnel are present
